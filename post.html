Why I Started This Blog

> June 12, 2026

I am a 25-year-old young man studying physics at university. Not long ago, I discovered the world of coding — building programs, writing systems, making machines do intelligent things. That discovery changed everything.

I am now deeply determined to build a system — a real, working program — that can fetch live data from the internet, clean and organize it, analyze it, and generate actionable intelligence that can be traded upon.

Not a toy. Not a demo. A system that works.

This blog is the public record of that journey. Every lesson learned, every breakthrough, every setback. Physics gave me the discipline to think in systems. Code is giving me the tools to build them. Markets will be where I prove it.

The goal is a hundred million dollars. The timeline is whatever it takes. The work starts now.
